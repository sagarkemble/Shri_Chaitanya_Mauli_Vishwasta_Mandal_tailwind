ABOUT US

This orgnization Established in 1982, is a registered charitable trust working for under privilaged sector of society.Engaged in dedicatedly providing Holistic Daycare While leading Sanskar-varg Effectively and Empowering women

Our Mission

To offer, high-quality daycare services that support the development and well-being for children from low-income families.To lead Sanskar-varg-To nurture children into resposible citizens of India by lending a hand for their physical,mental, intellectual,social and emotional development

What we do

Daycare

A safe, stimulating space to nurture young minds crucial for children's early development.Helps to grow, learn, and thrive while parents focus on their work with peace of mind.

Sanskar Varga

Shaping character and behaviour for a better future!
Empowering children with values, ethics, and integrity today for brighter, kinder citizens of tomorrow.

Self help group

Empowering Women!
Transforming communities! Uniting skills, building confidence, and creating financial independence for a better future.

Study center

Enhancing academic performance and personal development
Helping students unlock their potential and create pathways to success.

Library

Encouraging education personal growth study and reflection
A world of knowledge by making books and resources accessible to all in one place

Vocational training

Imparting skills for success, paths to prosperity! Equipping young ones with practical expertise, bridging the gap between education and employment.

Counseling center

Guidance and support!
A safe space to nurture mental well-being, empowering individuals to overcome challenges and thrive in life.

Immunization center

Protect today, be healthy tomorrow!
Help to safeguard health , prevent diseases,reduce outbreaks and contribute to immunity of community
