About Us

It was some 40 yers back, The Chaitanya Mauli Vishwast mandal was established by an entrepreneur Shri. Vaman Shankar Joshi alongwith his wife, Malati Joshi and some of their like minded friends, with Vision to ,Develop morally upright, responsible and cultured future generation, who would contribute in upliftment of their own brothers and sisters, as well as for betterment of society and Nation.
Empowering Children Shaping Future

What made the Origin of Day-care?

When Shri. Vaman Shankar Joshi alongwith his wife Malati Joshi planned to recide in Panchsheel Nagar, they observed that, the area was housed by hard working, hand to mouth labours, facing challenging social conditions. Consequently the children growing up in this area were facing various difficulties. Some were drawn in alcohol, tobacco, gutkha addiction, gambling and other social vices. In order to develop good character among the children in vicinity and ensuring better future to them, by providing them education and fostering in them values, The Chaitanya Mauli Vishwast mandal trust was established.
Let your smile change the world

Transforming Lives for 40 Years

Over the long time of past 40 years, the NGO has successfully supported more than 10,000 children and students from the needy class through various social and educational activities. Many of these students have excelled in their lives and careers as teachers, social workers, doctors, engineers, entrepreneurs and government employees. They continue to maintain a connection with Chaitanya Mauli.
